The Problem

Sound Familiar?

Most growing companies hit the same wall. The revenue motion exists — but nobody can see it, measure it, or trust it.

No Dashboard Exists

Leadership makes decisions based on gut feel because there's no real-time view of pipeline, revenue, or forecast.

HubSpot Is a Mess

Duplicate contacts, blank fields, inconsistent pipeline stages. Nobody trusts the data because nobody owns it.

Reporting Takes Hours

Every Monday someone exports CSVs and patches them together in a spreadsheet. That's not a system — that's a liability.

Marketing and Sales Don't Align

Marketing measures clicks. Sales measures calls. Nobody can tie activity to closed revenue because the attribution doesn't exist.

No Forecast Process

Month-end surprises, gut-check numbers, and no visibility into gap-to-quota until it's too late to course correct.

Leads Fall Through the Cracks

No routing, no automation, no follow-up tasks. Leads sit unassigned for days while competitors answer the phone.
